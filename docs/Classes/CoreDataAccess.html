CoreDataAccess

public final class CoreDataAccess : DataAccess

Presides over the setup and management of the entire Core Data stack, along with managing the lifecycle of background context operations. Background contexts use the NSMergeByPropertyStoreTrumpMergePolicy merge policy, and will share a persistent store with the mainContext, and change synchronization between the contexts are performed by NSPersistentContainer.
